# J.Crew Group (jcrew-group)
J.Crew Group, Inc. is an American multi-brand specialty retailer offering women's, men's, and children's apparel, shoes, and accessories under the J.Crew, Madewell, and Crewcuts brands. The company operates retail stores, factory outlet stores, and direct-to-consumer e-commerce sites.
